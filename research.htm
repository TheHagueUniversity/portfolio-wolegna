In the first module studying UXD I had to choose a subculture that I would not be a part of, for my Research for Design course and study it to find what its pains or needs are and how can they be accomplished.


I decided to go for international students that play football, as I am not a football fan myself. I then started looking for potential participants in my interviews, and after succesfully carrying 2 interviews I also went on and observed how a game of futsal unfolds and had a few informal discussions with some of the people playing.


After defining what are the possible frustrations of the students that play football or futsal, I came to the realisation that the biggest issue for them is organizing games outside of school.

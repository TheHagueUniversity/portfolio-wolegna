Education


One of the first projects I did was creating a Creative Toolbox App prototype, that would integrate Creative Techniques, for both Diverging and Converging.


The role of the app is to make it easy for a designer, and not only, to access creative techniques that would be just a few taps away. The app offers the sense of accomplishment by allowing the user to add new techniques as they learn them.


The prototype would show different capabilities of the app, like having dark-mode. I avoided using a search icon in my prototype, as the number of techniques present is a low one, and they are already split by categories.
